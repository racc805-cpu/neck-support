⚠️ Waking up stiff? It may not be your mattress.

The real reason your pillow can make your neck feel worse.

Most pillows look “soft,” but they often don’t keep your neck aligned for hours. That small mismatch can lead to pressure, stiffness, and restless sleep.

  • Too high / too flat can push your neck into an awkward angle.
  • Wrong support can let your head sink and strain one side.
  • “Soft” isn’t the same as ergonomic—alignment matters more.
🔎
Quick tip: If you wake up and immediately rub your neck or rotate your shoulders to “loosen up,” your pillow support is a likely suspect.
